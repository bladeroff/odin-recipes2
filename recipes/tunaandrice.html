Tuna and Rice

Ingredients

  • Can of tuna
  • 2 Min microwave rice

Recomended Condiments

  • Mayonaise
  • Soy sauce
  • Chilli flakes
  • Spring onion
  • Sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Tear 20mm hole in bag of rice
  2. Throw rice into microwave for 2 minutes
  3. Open can of tuna with sharp object and drain liquid
  4. Stop microwave with 1 second remaining
  5. Throw rice into bowl followed by tuna
  6. Get creative with recommended condiments
  7. Consume
