Cornedbeef

Ingredients

  • Tin of corned beef
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Tin of spaghetti
  • Tin of coconut cream/milk
  • Red onion

Steps

  1. Heat pan over medium-high heat
  2. Dice red onion into palatable pieces
  3. Open all cans
  4. Throw in cornedbeef and fy until fat melts
  5. Throw in tomatoes until it starts to bubble, stir when the urge comes
  6. Once bubbling has commenced, add in coconut cream
  7. Add spaghetti and red onion, stir only a couple more times so the spaghetti stays in tact
  8. Blow, then consume. Bon apple teeth.
