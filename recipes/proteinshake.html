Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 100g Oats
  • Scoop of protein
  • Banana
  • Tbsp of peanut butter
  • 5g creatine
  • 500ml Full fat milk

Steps

  1. Oats into blender,blend until fine powder
  2. Add other powders
  3. Scoop as much peanut butter as you can fit onto your tbsp, then throw in the blender
  4. Peel banana, then use sharpest edge of tbsp to divide into roughly 10mm pieces. (this method saves washing a knife)
  5. Throw banana in blender
  6. Dont throw milk in blender, pour it
  7. Blend until you have a smooth consistency
  8. Down it in one
